Subscribe to the V&A's event calendar

Google calendar or Apple ical both allow you to 'subscribe' to calendars; these show up in your calendar just like appointments you have created for yourself - only you can't edit these of course.

  • The ical URL that you will need is: vam-cal.appspot.com/ical
